Baby's New Gear

Strollers and Car seats

Buying your stroller and car seat together is generally a good idea so you can be sure that the car seat attaches to the stroller properly. We went with a jogging stroller so we can take baby on trail hikes.

Cribs and Changing Station

This all important baby furniture will be apart of yours and baby's daily lives pick something that is functional and esteticaly pleasing. We found great deals on craigslist.

Breast pumps and Nursing Pillows

Breast feeding is all important and keeping the milk flowing is important as well. We decided to go with a Medela breast pump. We read many articles and all said Medela was the best.

Places to buy baby gear.

After hours of watching my lovely lady shop I have deduced that these websites have the best gear at the most reasonable prices.

☞ Babies R Us
Babies R us has agreat selection and they love to send you catalogs with useful coupons.
☞ Target
Targets website is suprisingly easy to set up a baby shower registry. They also have a great selection of strollers and car seats. If you want Eddie Bower this is the only store that carries that brand.
☞ Diapers.com
Diapers.com has everything and the kitchen sink for your baby with some really great deals. Plus next day delivery is awesome.
☞ Sears
Sears has a great selection of baby gear. Generally I think of Sears as a place to buy tools but they have a great baby selection.
