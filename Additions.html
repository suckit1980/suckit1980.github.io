How your apps may betray you

Within every app you run on your cell phone or PC there are hidden codes that may give hackers details of your life. Within a downloaded app there may be a piece of code "geolocation" this code gives your current location to the app. When using google earth or trying to find a resturant a "geolocation" function is great. At the same time someone could be tracking you looking for an optimal time to rob your house. Take web security serious it can save you time, money, and much more.
